It supports FDG (metabolic) and Florbetapir (amyloid) tracers allows for the automatic quantification of PET tracer binding in anatomical subregions. Using a paired NeuroQuant compatible T1-weighted MRI PET tracer binding is accurately localized to identified brain structures.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column width="1/2"][vc_column_text]
