<h3 style="text-align: left;">About CorTechs Labs</h3>

<hr />

<div class=""></div>
<div class="wpb_text_column wpb_content_element ">
<div class="wpb_wrapper">

CorTechs Labs develops and markets breakthrough medical device software solutions capable of automatically segmenting and quantifying brain structures, making quantitative analysis of MRI images of the human brain a routine part of clinical practice. CorTechs Labs’ cutting-edge brain imaging analysis provides neurologists, radiologists, and clinical researchers worldwide with a convenient and cost-effective means to quantify subcortical structures to help assess a variety of neurological conditions, such as Alzheimer’s disease, epilepsy, multiple sclerosis, brain trauma and brain development.
